about

Building the web, one clean line at a time.

I'm a web developer based in Lagos, Nigeria, focused on turning simple ideas into polished, professional websites. My work centers on the businesses people interact with every day — cafés, restaurants, hotels, studios, and agencies — and making sure their first impression online matches the quality of what they offer in person.

I write clean, semantic HTML, style with modern CSS (Flexbox, Grid, animations), and bring pages to life with JavaScript. I care about the details most visitors never consciously notice — spacing, load speed, mobile behavior — because that's what separates a site that looks fine from one that feels right.

"Good design is invisible. It just makes people trust what they're looking at."
